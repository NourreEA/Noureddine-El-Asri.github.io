About me

Hi there. I'm Noureddine El-Asri, a PhD student in cryptography at the LITIS laboratory, Rouen Normandy University. I'm working on symmetric cryptography under Valentin Suder1 and Magali Bardet2. I'm interested in applications of finite fields in cryptography, especially in odd characteristic. Below are some academic information about me.
